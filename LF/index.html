You are what you eat

Each portion of London Falafel achieves that elusive, industry-defining balance of being healthy, tasty and prime quality

Perfect Dietary Balance

Our falafel is the artful combination of premium ingredients and expert know-how. Each bite is high in protein, fibre and other healthy nutrients, and is very, very low in fat. We only use the highest quality chickpeas and broad beans, a long list of delicious vegetables, the freshest herbs and over a dozen spices. We only make food that we would be happy for our own children to eat.

Allergen Free. Natural. Vegan

Our falafel is free from wheat, dairy, gluten, eggs, soya, nuts and any artificial ingredients or additives. Everything that goes into London Falafel is 100% natural, 100% healthy, 100% vegan. We have very stringent controls and checks to make sure our quality is impeccable.

The Environment and Animals

London Falafel only produces vegetarian and vegan foods. That means we don't damage our environment or hurt animals.

Pull up a chair. Have a taste. Come and join us.
Life is endlessly delicious with our falafel!

As a sandwich: In a hot grill pan, mark and warm flatbread for 1 minute on each side. Remove and add 2 to 3 falafels to each warm flatbread. Top with some arugula (rocket), tomato, onion and smoky tahini sauce. Heaven on earth!

Enjoy as part of a meze: London Falafel tastes delicious on its own. However, with other meze dishes that complement its flavour, its taste reaches new heights. We suggest you try our falafel with babaganoush, broad bean salad and hummus. It is amazing!

Wrap it: Why not try a wrap full of herbs, vegetables, our special sauce and falafel… Yum!

Locally Sourced Ingredients

London Falafel uses locally sourced prime vegetables, herbs and oil. There are several reasons for this. We want to have a positive impact on our immediate environment and benefit our neighbours. We want to use the freshest and highest quality ingredients. We want to reduce our already low carbon footprint. The end result will be happy local suppliers, a stable environment and happy customers.

Quality Controls

We have a very strict quality code: We only make food that we are happy for our children to eat: our creations are all natural, healthy and of the best quality. In order to achieve this, we have adopted internationally recognised stringent quality standards, including BRC Global, ISO 22000 and IFS, and we have regular external checks for compliance. In addition, we undertake regular internal quality inspections to maintain the highest product and service quality.
