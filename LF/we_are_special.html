We Are Special

Expert gastronomic know-how, dedicated R&D team, wide distribution and Europe's largest state of the art falafel production facility: that's why we are the leader in the World of Falafel!

Perfect Dietary Balance

Our falafel is the artful combination of premium ingredients and expert know-how. Each bite is high in protein, fibre and other healthy nutrients, and is very, very low in fat. We only use the highest quality chickpeas and broad beans, a long list of delicious vegetables, the freshest herbs and over a dozen spices. We only make food that we would be happy for our own children to eat.

Research

We carry out regular extensive market research in each locality where we have clients. This focuses on everything from changing culinary tastes to what our end customers are looking for now, from changing market trends to what our clients require to be more successful and in a position to respond to end-customer needs and wants. Our R&D process and the constant feedback loop we have established with our clients leads to product innovation that delivers first-class results.

Product Innovation & Testing

Our Product Development Team constantly innovates in terms of products and flavours, leaving end customers very happy and creating loyal, repeat customers. London Falafel is the result of a rigorous gastronomic process and customer focus groups. We also develop complementary products and various flavours to enhance the main line item, therefore helping our clients to maximise customer satisfaction and profitability. For instance, we produce two special sauces and hummus, with options of conventional, organic and omega-3 enhanced falafel.

Sourcing and Sustainability

Our Procurement Team scours the globe for the highest quality superfood nutrients, exotic tastes and freshest ingredients. We only work with producers that provide the highest quality products and integrated sustainability in their business practices. We never compromise on quality and positive environmental impact principles. This is simply because top quality products and highly effective business habits result in longevity and consistent growth both for your company and the London Falafel brand.

Production

There are myriad ways of making falafel and other food items. However, there is only one way to make London Falafel so that it is the best falafel you and your customers will ever taste. Our Production Team consists of highly skilled individuals who have produced an innovative production line that has an impressive throughput without ever compromising on quality, taste or health. From handling ingredients to forming and frying, from packaging to storing the end product, we have perfected the art of falafel making. You are more than welcome to visit our facilities to see our production first-hand.

Storage

Depending on our customers’ needs, we have several ways to refrigerate and store our products. Whether it is chilled, frozen or ambient, our Sales Team will meet your needs. We keep a small amount of stock in our facilities for emergencies, quick restocking and new clients. Otherwise, all our products are freshly made for you in response to orders. Our Sales Team and Production Team work in tandem to ensure you are never without London Falafel no matter how many times you want to restock.
